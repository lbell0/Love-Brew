Description Document - Bello & Pomponio

Our Product
With our web application, online dating users will now be able to gain insight on their profile and gauge how their responses appeal to the platform’s users.
Our Approach
By establishing a service that enables users to receive insight onto their profiles, this will better position their ability to make themselves more marketable to the platform users that he/she is interested in connecting with.
Our primary analysis will derive from biographies and question responses.
Biographies
This will be an open forum for users to write a biography of his/her choice in a textbox. Users will then have the ability to Like or Dislike the bio and understand which type of specified intent the user will match with.

Question Responses
Users will have two, prewritten questions to respond in which he/she can approach the response with a selected type of response.

Getting Feedback
Platform users will be prompted with individual’s profiles containing their biography and responses. From there, the viewing user will react to the prompted field in which we will gather the data. These reactions will consist of brief, preassigned metrics.
